## 1. Some important notes for the BFGS-Algorithm: (any mathematical symbols will be represented in the LaTeX language for a better reading)
- The BFGS-optimization problem is to minimize a differentiable scalar function f: \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}^n. In my example I simply use an easy shape of the at least two-times continuously differentiable Rosenbrock function.
- My code is free for coping and changing the inputs of the algorithm.
- The amount of iterations depends on the starting point you choose.
- Feel free for message me for any questions you all might have. Have fun with the algorithm.
